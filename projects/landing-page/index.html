Super Mario 🌟

    Mario

    Here's the heroic plumber you know and love! The main protector of the Mushroom Kingdom, Mario is always ready to fight for Princess Peach. He is the brother of Luigi. Bowser, the king of the Koopas, is no match for Mario, especially when he teams up with his friends!

    Luigi

    Here's our green loving mustachioed hero! Although not as brave as Mario, Luigi still manages to fight his way through haunted mansions. He is the taller of the two heroic brothers. Teamed up with his brother Mario, there's nothing he can't do!

    Princess Peach

    The beloved princess of the Mushroom Kingdom. No damsel in distress here! Princess Peach is capable of attacking enemies with turnips and pans and can hover over very far distances with her iconic dress. She is dedicated to serving and protecting the citizens of the Mushroom Kingdom and calls on Mario to help her once in a while.

    Bowser

    King of the Koopas and 'future' king of the Mushroom Kingdom (if he ever gets his way)! Ferocious, explosive, and filled with love for Princess Peach, (wait, what?) Bowser is not one to be messed with. He, his son, the Koopalings, and all of his minions are out to get Mario and friends so he can take over the Mushroom Kingdom!
